Step 1:

Goto the URL. After that click on "Create New App" button as shown in the figure.

Step 2:

Fill out the form by giving your App name, Description of your App, complete url address of your website and Call-back url which is optional. As shown in figure.

Step 3:

Proceed on the same page, then check the Developers agreement and then click "Create your Twitter application" button. As shown in figure.

Step 4:

Click on “Keys and Access Tokens Tab”. Where you will find "consumer key" and "Consumer secret". Proceed on the same page. As shown in figure.

Step 5:

Then click "Create my access token" button to create your access keys. As shown in figure.

Step 6:

Here you will find "Access Token" and "Access Token Secret". As shown in figure.
