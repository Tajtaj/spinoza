1 Creating new Twitter App:

1.1 Click on "Create New App" button at apps.twitter.com as shown below.

1.2 Fill out the form by giving your App name, Description of your App, complete url address of your website and Call-back url which is optional. As shown in figure.

1.3 check term and conditions checkbox and click on "Create your Twitter Application" button.

1.4 On next page, get your app keys from "Keys and Access Tokens" tab.

1.5 Generate your account access token by clicking "Create my access token" button.

1.6 Copy your access tokens as shown below.

2 Environment setup:

2.1 On Windows:
Control Panel -> System and Security -> System -> Advanced system settings -> Environment variables -> New

2.2 On Linux:

vi /.bash_profile OR vi /.bashrc TSAK_CONF=PATH to tsak.propertise file (make sure directory have read permissions) source /.bash_profile OR source /.bashrc echo $TSAK_CONF
